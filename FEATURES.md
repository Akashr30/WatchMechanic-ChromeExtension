# Time Zone Converter - Features

A simple Chrome extension to help you work with times across different time zones.

---

## What Can This Extension Do?

1. **See Current Time in Multiple Time Zones**  
   View the current time in different countries side by side — India, USA, Japan, Europe, and more.

2. **Copy Current Unix Timestamp**  
   One-click copy of the current Unix timestamp (in milliseconds or seconds).

3. **Convert Unix Timestamps**  
   Paste any Unix timestamp and see what date/time it represents in all your selected time zones.

4. **Convert Time Between Time Zones**  
   Enter a specific date and time in one timezone, and see what it would be in other time zones.

5. **Double-Click to Convert Timestamps on Any Webpage**  
   Double-click on any timestamp you see on a webpage, and a quick tooltip shows the converted time in your chosen timezones.

6. **Copy Times with One Click**  
   Click on any time card to copy the time to your clipboard in your preferred format.

7. **Choose Your Copy Format**  
   Pick how you want times copied — ISO format (with timezone offset), US format, EU format, or others.

8. **Add Custom Time Zones**  
   Add any city or region from around the world that's not in the default list.

9. **Set Your Primary Time Zone**  
   Mark your main timezone so it always appears first.

10. **Daylight Saving Time Aware**  
    Automatically adjusts for daylight saving time in regions that observe it.

---

*Perfect for remote teams, travelers, developers, and anyone working across time zones!*
